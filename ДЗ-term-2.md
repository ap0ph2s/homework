### 2 Какая альтернатива без pipe команде ```grep <some_string> <some_file> | wc -l```?
---
Либо
```
grep <some_string> <some_file> > <temp_file>
wc -l <temp_file>
```
либо
```grep  <some_string> <some_file> -c```
